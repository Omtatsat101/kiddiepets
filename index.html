Frequently Asked Questions

Everything families want to know before buying.

Are your products safe for young children (under 3)?+
Yes. Every product we carry is reviewed for small-part hazards, non-toxic materials, and sharp edges before we list it. Products that aren't safe for under-3s are clearly labeled. When in doubt, check the product page — safety info is always in the description.
Do you ship outside the US?+
We currently ship within the US and Canada. International shipping is coming soon — join our email list to be notified when your country goes live.
How do I know which starter kit is right for my family?+
Start with the type of pet you're getting (Puppy, Kitten, or Small Pet). Each kit includes all the basics for the first 30 days — bedding, food bowls, a toy, and essentials. If you're unsure, our site agent (bottom right) can help you pick based on your pet and kids' ages.
What's your return policy?+
30-day hassle-free returns on all products. If your pet doesn't love it or it doesn't fit, send it back for a full refund. Used pet beds or opened food items are not eligible, but everything else is covered. No restocking fee.
When does free shipping kick in?+
Free standard shipping on orders over $35. Most starter kits qualify automatically. For orders under $35, flat-rate shipping of $4.99 applies. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.
Can I buy the kid + pet matching sets separately?+
Yes! The matching bandana sets and pajama sets are sold as bundles (kid + pet together) but you can also request the kid-only or pet-only size at checkout via the notes field. We'll do our best to accommodate.
Are your treats and food products natural / organic?+
Most of our treat range is made from natural ingredients with no artificial colors or flavors. We clearly label products with grain-free, limited-ingredient, or organic certifications where applicable. Check individual product pages for full ingredient lists.
